My Current Research
Skin Analysis by Sulcus Cutis Extraction

My research involves studying ways to automate the analysis of skin conditions and potential early skin disease prediction. Having started not too long ago, not much progress has been made yet.

I am currently in the process of looking for ways to separate the little "sections" of skin that is defined by the lines which run along the skin. Current methods being tested is by using local normalization on an image of skin with black ink. I then attempt to segment the image using the sections using watershed and ridge detection.
